# Stallion Monetization & Go-To-Market Strategy

## Product Summary
- **Name:** Stallion (ACE Replacement)
- **What it does:** Generates C82 XML customs declarations for T&T ASYCUDA
- **Target:** Customs brokers, freight forwarders, logistics operators in Trinidad & Tobago

## Direct Sales Strategy

### Target Market (T&T)
1. **Primary:** Licensed customs brokers (estimate 30-50 in T&T)
2. **Secondary:** Freight forwarding companies (20-30)
3. **Tertiary:** Import/export traders doing own paperwork (larger, fewer)

### Sales Process
1. **Research:** Build list of licensed brokers via T&T Customs Authority
2. **Warm outreach:** 
   - LinkedIn connection + value-first message
   - Direct email with case for why they need this
3. **Demo:** Free 15-min screen share showing value
4. **Pilot offer:** 3-month trial at 50% discount
5. **Expand:** Upsell to subscription after pilot success
